# CODE OF CONDUCT

As contributors and maintainers of this project, we pledge to respect all people who contribute through issues, pull requests, or otherwise, and to follow this code of conduct in all our interactions within the project community.

## 1. Be welcoming and inclusive.

We welcome and encourage participation in our project regardless of experience level, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, nationality, or other similar characteristics.

## 2. Be respectful and considerate.

We value respect and consideration in all interactions, and will not tolerate discrimination, harassment, or any other form of disrespectful behavior.

## 3. Be constructive and collaborative.

We encourage constructive criticism, feedback, and discussion, and welcome collaboration with all contributors. We strive to make our project a safe and supportive environment for all.

## 4. Follow the project guidelines.

All contributors must follow the project guidelines and contribute in a way that is consistent with the project's objectives and mission.

## 5. Take responsibility for your actions.

All contributors are responsible for their actions and words within the project community, and should strive to resolve conflicts in a respectful and constructive manner.

## 6. Report unacceptable behavior.

If you witness or experience behavior that violates this code of conduct, please report it to the project maintainers at [INSERT EMAIL ADDRESS]. We will take all reports seriously and respond promptly to ensure a safe and supportive environment for all contributors.

By contributing to this project, you agree to abide by this code of conduct. We reserve the right to remove any contributor who violates these guidelines or who is otherwise disruptive to the project community.
